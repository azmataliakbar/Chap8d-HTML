Understand the portfolio!

Why it's special: A portfolio is special because it showcases your skills, achievements, and projects, serving as a visual resume that highlights your expertise. It's a powerful tool to leave a lasting impression on potential clients, employers, or collaborators.
What it's about: A portfolio is about presenting your best work in a structured and engaging way, demonstrating your abilities, creativity, and experience. It typically includes samples of completed projects, case studies, personal statements, and testimonials. It helps others understand your style, proficiency, and the value you bring. Whether you're an artist, developer, designer, or writer, a portfolio acts as your professional showcase. It's an essential way to build credibility and attract opportunities.
